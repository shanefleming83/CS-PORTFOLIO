What This Project Was

This project was a time organizer for homework. This can be used for all students at all ages at all levels of learning.

The Problem or Question

The solution my project was trying to reach was the achievement of organizing a student's time after school. I wanted people to be able to quickley and accurately figure out how much time thye had to spend on their work after school.

The Data, Features, or Labels Involved

There were a few questions in a survey that I had 40-50 people take and they went along the lines of, how much homework do you have, how much time does one peice take, then how much for two, how much for three, and so on to 5+ peices of homework.

What the AI System Was Supposed to Do

It was trying to predict and outline how much time you will spend on homework after school.

One Limitation, Bias Risk, or Ethical Concern

One concern with this project is that expectations on speed of you doing homework might be wrong, because it was trained with mostly honors level students.

One Way a Human Decision Mattered

If a human decides to answer questions the AI asks wildly then the AI will get confused and likely give the human an inaccurate answer. The decision to answer the questions with intent and not as a joke makes the difference of whether the AI gives a bad answer and a really accurate one.
