The Internet, Cloud, Cybersecurity, or Privacy Concept Explained

The concept this poster teaches is cybersecurity. It teaches how to detect when someone is trying to scam you and how to protect and secure yourself from it.

The Risk or Threat Involved

Anyone who can't read or can not follow directions may be affected. They could accidentally take the advice and misunderstand it and do everything opposite.

At Least Two Defenses

Two real ways to prevent or reduce the risk. "Be careful" does not count.

  1. Defense 1: Check The Sender - If it isn't someone you know or the actual website's email then it's likely a scam.
  2. Defense 2: Never Click The Link - Check the source first, if the domain or website name is wrong it is a scam and when you click it you give it permission to your data.

Why Human Choices Matter

The human decision decides whether or not to go on a scetchy website or to click the link in an email and a computer can't stop that. But a person can also make better decisions to protect one's computer, privacy, and data.
