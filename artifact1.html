What This Was Supposed to Communicate

My project was trying to communicate the success, history, current positioning, and culture of each of the Philidelphia sports teams.

Who My Audience Was

The intended audience of the project was sports fans that wanted a deeper look into Philidelphia sports.

One Design Choice I Made on Purpose

I chose to do the project on Philly sports teams because I wanted my audience to know the incredible history, success, and culture behind the teams that most don't know.

One Thing I Would Improve

I believe that the only thing I could change is the depth of the information. If I had the time I could make the info much more impactful and complete.
